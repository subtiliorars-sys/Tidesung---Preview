Tidesung

A meditative whale-song experience exploring communication and connection in the ocean.

🌊 One Journey. One Song. No Words Needed.

A single, perfect moment: you are a young whale on a solo journey through the deep. Using whale songs, you interact with the ocean around you — calling to creatures, triggering bioluminescence, feeling the pull of migration and belonging.

▶ Experience Demo ⭐ Star on GitHub 🐋 Coming Soon

🎵 Song as Language

No dialogue, no text. Communication happens through whale vocalizations — a universal language of melody.

✨ Bioluminescent World

Your songs trigger light in the darkness. The ocean responds to your presence in shimmering, living color.

🌅 Migration & Belonging

Feel the pull of ancient routes. Discover your place in a vast, interconnected community of ocean beings.

🎧 Meditative Play

No combat. No fail states. No pressure. Just beauty, music, and the deep ocean at your own pace.

What You'll Experience

  • 25–35 minutes of uninterrupted, narrative-driven exploration
  • A living ocean ecosystem that responds to your songs
  • Whale vocalizations grounded in real cetacean communication research
  • An ambient soundscape inspired by the deep sea
  • A story told without words — through music, light, and motion

💡 Themes

Connection Without Words: Communicating across difference without translation
Solitude & Community: Being alone while never truly separate
Beauty & Wonder: Finding awe in the natural world
Journey & Return: The oldest story — leaving home, finding yourself, coming back
"Inspired by Flower (thatgamecompany) and real whale communication research — a short, meditative game about the ocean, music, and belonging."
