# Tidesung — Preview

A meditative whale-song experience exploring communication and connection in the ocean.

## What is Tidesung?

A single, perfect moment: you are a young whale on a solo journey through the deep. Using whale songs, you interact with the ocean around you — calling to creatures, triggering bioluminescence, feeling the pull of migration and belonging.

No combat. No fail states. Just beauty, music, and the ocean.

**Duration:** 25–35 minutes  
**Inspired by:** *Flower* (thatgamecompany), real whale communication research  
**Genre:** Meditative, artistic experience
