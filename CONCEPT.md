# Tidesung — Concept Document

## One Perfect Moment

A young whale takes its first solo journey into the deep ocean. Armed only with its voice — whale songs that resonate through water — it discovers the vastness of the world, encounters beauty and wonder, and learns that it is never alone.

This is *Tidesung*: a meditative experience about discovery, connection, and belonging.

## Core Concept

**What if your only interaction with the world was your voice?**

In Tidesung, whale song is both navigation and emotion. Sing, and the ocean responds: creatures are drawn to you, currents shift, bioluminescence blooms. Through song, you discover that communication transcends language — it is pure emotional understanding.

## Inspiration

- **Flower** (thatgamecompany) — meditative gameplay, no fail states, environmental beauty
- **Abzu** — underwater exploration, creature connection, wordless narrative
- **Real Whale Science** — recent breakthroughs in cetacean communication (AI analysis of whale phonemes, humpback "dialects," emotional vocalizations)

## Target Audience

- Players seeking contemplative, artistic experiences
- Ocean and whale enthusiasts
- Educational interest in cetacean communication
- Anyone who loved Flower, Journey, or similar artistic games

## Key Themes

- **Loneliness → Connection:** Journey from isolation to belonging
- **Voice & Expression:** Communication as identity
- **Wonder & Discovery:** The joy of encountering beauty for the first time
- **Nature's Intelligence:** Respect for whale cognition and emotion

## Audio-First Design

Tidesung is built around real whale vocalizations. The game uses authentic whale song data converted into interactive mechanics — making the player feel what it's like to sing as a whale.

## Duration & Scope

- **25–35 minutes** for one full playthrough
- Single, continuous ocean location
- Small but perfectly crafted experience
- Replayable for emotional resonance (like Flower)
